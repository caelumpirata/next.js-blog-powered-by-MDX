## 🎞️ DCI Aspect Ratios:

{/* 
Resolution	Aspect Ratio	Use Case
4096 × 2160	≈1.90∶1	Full Frame
4096 × 1716	≈2.39∶1	CinemaScope Crop
3996 × 2160	≈1.85∶1	Flat Crop */}

1. **Full Frame** - `4096 × 2160` (≈1.90∶1) - Experience immersive cinematography. 🎬🎥
2. **CinemaScope Crop** - `4096 × 1716` (≈2.39∶1) - Unleash widescreen cinematic brilliance. 🌆🎞️
3. **Flat Crop** - `3996 × 2160` (≈1.85∶1) - Perfect for wide flat-screen displays. 📺🎦

## 🖥️ UHD Aspect Ratios:
{/* Resolution	Aspect Ratio	Use Case
3840 × 2160	16∶9	Full Frame
3840 × 1600	≈2.40∶1	CinemaScope Crop */}

1. **Full Frame** - `3840 × 2160` (16∶9) - Ideal for high-res home entertainment. 🏠🎮
2. **CinemaScope Crop** - `3840 × 1600` (≈2.40∶1) - Elevate your movie nights. 🌌🍿
